Hi All! I am an undergraduate student working under the guidance of Dr. Dongmei Li at the University of Rochester Medical Center. I am also an undergraduate researcher supervised by Dr. Chenliang Xu, in Computer Science department at University of Rochester, engaging in creating machine learning models to understand human scene perception from multi-modal inputs and adversarial machine learning.
